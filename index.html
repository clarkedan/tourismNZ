How it all works

  1. Choose a pick up and drop off location (can't be the same), icons on the map will show your pick up and drop off location as well as charging stations along the way.
  2. Verify how many days you wish to be traveling using our electric car hire and tour.
  3. Identify how many people will be traveling in the electric vehicle (including the driver).
  4. Select an electric vehicle to hire and proceed.

HIRING CONDITIONS:

  • MOTORCYCLE: 1 passanger, 1 day min - 5 day max hire.
  • COUPE: 1-2 passangers, 1 day min - 10 day max hire.
  • HATCH: 1-5 passangers, 3 day min - 10 day max hire.
  • FAMILY WAGON: 2-6 passangers, 1 day min - 5 day max hire.
